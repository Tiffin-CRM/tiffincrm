How to Use Tiffin CRM?
Understand How to use every function & usecase.
Understand Tiffin CRM Working
Know How the app function & process the data
General Information
All the active orders will shown in deliveries & home screen as per the current time and your action on last time deliveries.
Active orders exclude the order of customer who paused their account or meal plan or cancelled the specific deliveries.
Preparing the order mean deducting the balance from customers virtual wallet.
You can manage individual deliveries as well as from delivery tab
You can pause all the future deliveries or cancel any specific delivery of any customer.
Customer can login to imeals.in and also pause/resume all future deliveries if he isn’t blocked.
Your daily work is to start preparing and update their delivery status individually or click on deliver all button.
If any customer want to cancel or pause their account. make sure it is reflecting on your side as well before starting preparation.
You can click “skip all deliveries” button in case of any non working day. it will not deduct balances of your customers until you prepare food for them.
Best Practices
Use it daily to avoid data corruption.
Incase you still missed. You can go to backdate and deduct balances of customers in one click.
How to See what Items to Buy?
You can see it on daily bases for future days or for specific time on any specify day that today.
General Information
On Home Screen Click on Time Drop down and select All Day
Now Check Item to prepare table and estimate the raw material as per the item to prepare for the whole day.
you can change the dates and check it for tomorrow or any other individual day.
How to see What & How much to Prepare?
By default You will see it on time specific but you can also see it on daily bases and plan the preparation accordingly. You will also see the total count on top of the screen for how much orders are pending, paused and cancelled.
General Information
You can use the time filter or by default you will see the items to prepare for that time if previous orders are completed or skipped.
Roti and other countable items are shown exactly how much to prepare in pieces and other as servings.
In items to prepare screen Veg and Non Veg Tabs are related to customer not meal. you can prepare different items for veg/non veg customers accordingly.
How to Cancel Someone’s Order?
You can cancel orders before starting preparation. so customers who cancelled will not be charged.
General Information
Single or Specific Delivery Cancellations for any specific time or date can be cancelled via going to delivery tab and use pending filter and locate the customer via searching or exploring and click on cancel delivery option after selecting that card.
Stop All Orders until Information can be done by going to customer profile via customer tab and click on pause customer button. It can be resumed back from same location.
Temporary Stop All orders of Specific Time can be achieved via editing the meal plan and uncheck the active checkbox in the meal plan.
Permanent Stop All orders of Specific Time can be achieved via deleting the meal plan.
Permanent Stop All orders of Customer can be done via locating to customer profile and click on edit button and block the customer.
How to Start Preparation?
Start Preparation is done to deduct balance for all potential deliveries of customers. Paused/blocked Account,Inactive Meal Plan & Cancelled Deliveries will be excluded from that meal time data. After clicking on start preparation customer can’t be able to cancel their order but they can stop that delivery and it will be counted as wasted by customer and customer balance will not be refunded in this case. but you can manually refund if you wants.
How to see what to pack?
After Starting the preparation you will see this screen replacing items to prepare.
General Information
Here the grouping of items will changed now total 4 roti and 3 roti are also counted as different and as serving not pieces which make easy to pack.
You can also click on the item to know who wants it.
you can go to delivery and also see items to pack for individual.
How to manage deliveries?
You can assign delivery boy and they can access only pending deliveries on their Tiffin CRM app. You can perform the same action via your admin app.
General Information
In Deliveries section with pending delivery filter applied you can click on any card to select and perform the action like Mark as Delivered, Wasted, Refunded, or Disputed.
You can interchange the statuses as well except the refunded status.
You can also Click on Deliver All Button in Home screen to mark all as delivered.
Make sure to update the statuses from pending_delivery before start preparation for next meal time on that day to keep the data clean.
How customer can manage orders?
you can provide link to imeals.in website to customer via WhatsApp and they can login their with their mobile number and manage their orders.
General Information
Customer can Mark the Active Delivery (Aka orders which are in preparing status) as wasted. After that you don't need to deliver that order and no refund is provided. you can go to delivery tab, apply wasted filter and reverse the action or refund for delivery.
Customer can cancel upcoming deliveries and amount will not be deducted for that deliveries when you start preparation and his order information will be excluded from items to prepare and pending counts on home screen. you can go to delivery tab, apply canceled filter and reverse the action for that.
He can Temporary Stop Future Deliveries until you or himself turn it back.
He can Pause/Resume his meal Plans.
He can message you to support or edit items in their meal plan.
He can find you Upi id to pay you.
Best Practices
If Any Customer reach out to you to Stop any Delivery temporarily you can send the link to imeals.in so they can do that anytime and you save your time & energy on that.
How to See Customer Data?
You can use filter for tags, zones, status bases, inactive meals, Low Bal, & search box to see customers data in customer tab. You can also sort customer on bases of low balances, latest on top & Oldest on top.
How to Manage Finances?
To See Your finances navigate to finance tab. And Check Daily Revenue and Add Expenses.
